WVDI COMPANY PROFILE

Background

“The First and No. 1 Driving School in Negros Occidental”

Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp (WVDI), the first and number one driving school in Negros Occidental, is an LTO accredited driving school that has evolved to become the first to offer comprehensive packages including class lectures by a Tesda certified lecturer on defensive driving and preventive maintenance, on-site lecture (Battery, Lights, Oil, Water, Brake, Air, Gas, Spare tire maintenance), driving simulator and manual or automatic actual driving lessons by LTO certified driving instructors in Bacolod.

 

On August 3, 2019, Land Transportation Office (LTO) required all Student Driver Permit applicants to complete AT LEAST 15 hours of Theoretical Driving Course (TDC) and 8 hours of Practical Driving Course (PDC). In relation, Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp is the FIRST driving school to be authorized by LTO to practice this policy. Furthermore, it is also the FIRST Driving School in the Philippines approved by LTO Region VI to operate the Outreach Program of TDC and PDC in Negros Island.

Caring for your safety since 1997

From December 28, 2020 to October 2021, the staff and instructors organized the outreach programs in remote cities or municipalities in Negros to conduct the mandatory seminars for applicants who are unable to visit WVDI offices.

Prior to LTO’s memorandum, Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp has been training individuals to drive efficiently and to ensure that driver’s licenses are issued only to deserving applicants with sufficient driving skills and knowledge on road safety and proper road courtesy to minimize if not abate untoward traffic accidents.

From a single branch in 1997, Elda Barthelemy, Philippe Barthelemy and the staff successfully launched the additional branches to provide driving lessons services.

WVDI Mission

Our mission at Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp. is to empower individuals with the skills and knowledge necessary for safe and confident driving. We believe that driving is a life-long skill that requires continuous learning and improvement, and it is our commitment to provide the highest quality education in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Because we care for your safety, we go above and beyond to provide comprehensive packages that include defensive driving, preventive maintenance, and hands-on training. Our experienced and certified instructors use innovative teaching methods and state-of-the-art facilities to ensure that our students are prepared for the road ahead.

At Western Visayas Driving Institute Corp., we are dedicated to making the roads a safer place, one driver at a time. Join us in our mission to drive with confidence and peace of mind.
